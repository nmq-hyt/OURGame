Game Rules


1.0 Each player starts with 10 hp Each player starts with 1 energy per round, Energy must be spent by playing a card.(turn cannot be skiped even if the card played would do nothing) Turn begins with drawing 1 card from there deck, If the deck is empty the discard pile is shuffled and is placed on the deck spot, All cards cost one energy. A card is read from top down, Attack: pick an opponent and deal one damage, (multi opponents can be chose when more than one attack a card) Health: Regain 1 hp for each icon. Draw: draw one card form the deck for each icon, Energy: gain one energy for each icon on the crad. Shield/defended: the card is placed in front of the player and is able to take a hit for each shield icon on the card. Any damage dealt to a player with a shield will damage the shield instead. If you can still attack after a shield is broken the rest is taken by the player. Once a shield card is used up (all shield on card have been damaged) the card goes to the discard pile. Start of the game you draw 4 cards to start, On a player you draw a card, If a player runs out of cards in hand they draw 2 cards immediately, Game first player can start randomly(could have a dice roll on screen to determine) Hp cannot go over 10,
